WHAT WE SUPPORT

We provide a comprehensive service from statistical analysis of medical studies, interpretation of your results to academic counseling.

Medical Article & Research Design

The most important factor affecting the success of scientific research and medical articles is they have a strong design. The most important criticisms in the publication process of the studies are on this subject. By communicating with our experienced consultants during the planning phase, you can plan your work with an appropriate method and based on solid foundations.

Medical Statistics Reporting

The data of your studies are evaluated by our specialist physicians with a clinical epidemiology approach, your data is processed and reported in the most accurate way with tables, graphical representations and comments suitable for your study.

Academic Counseling

Academic level consultancy services are provided on sample size determination with power analysis, research design and method for scientific research that is intended to be turned into medical articles with M.Sc and Ph.D medical/dentistry theses, and medical articles.

Data Entry & Analysis

The database of your researches is prepared by our experienced experts by converting them into a database of statistical programs to be analyzed. Data analysis is done meticulously by selecting the most appropriate tests. It is given with the most appropriate table and graphic presentation, with clear, easy, understandable explanations and comments.

Power Analysis

Making your studies in the most appropriate sample size is possible with the power analysis to be done at the very beginning of the planning. As sample size is known, by using the same formulas considering default power levels according to sample size at the beggining of the study, if considered necessary, power calculation can be done reversibly at the end of the study.
